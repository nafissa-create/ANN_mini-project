# Context
This project was developed as part of a group assignment in the course Machine learning. The project demonstrates how artificial neural networks can be used to analyze mental health data and predict the likelihood of mental health conditions based on demographic, academic and lifestyle factors.

# Background and Motivation
Mental health challenges among young people and university students continue to increase worldwide. Early identification of individuals who may be at risk can assist institutions and healthcare providers in offering timely support and intervention. Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) are capable of learning complex relationships within data, making them suitable for predictive healthcare applications.

# Objective
The project aims to:
demonstrate the application of neural networks in healthcare analytics
evaluate the predictive performance of an ANN model
support data-driven decision making through machine learning techniques

# Dataset
The dataset contains information related to teenagers' mental health, including attributes such as:
Age
Gender
Academic Performance
Sleep Duration
Physical Activity
Social Media Usage
Family Support
Stress Level
Depression Indicators
Anxiety Indicators
Mental Health Status (Target Variable)
